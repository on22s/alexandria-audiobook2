Rain tapped against the observatory windows while Mara checked the locked door.

"Did you hear that?" Mara asked.

"Yes," Ilya said. "Stay behind me."

The old speaker crackled――then fell silent.

"No."

"No," Mara repeated, more firmly.

Ilya lowered his voice. "The password is café-au-lait; copy it exactly."

They kissed urgently, shed their clothes, and continued consensually into the bedroom.

Outside, the storm rolled over the empty road. No one followed them.
